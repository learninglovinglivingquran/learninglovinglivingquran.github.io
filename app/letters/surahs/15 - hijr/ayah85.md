# Surah Hijr Ayaat 85

# وَمَا خَلَقْنَا ٱلسَّمَـٰوَٰتِ وَٱلْأَرْضَ وَمَا بَيْنَهُمَآ إِلَّا بِٱلْحَقِّ ۗ وَإِنَّ ٱلسَّاعَةَ لَـَٔاتِيَةٌۭ ۖ فَٱصْفَحِ ٱلصَّفْحَ ٱلْجَمِيلَ

# Basic translation
And we did not create the heavens and the earth and what is between them except with purpose. And the hour is definitely coming, so forgive with a beautiful forgiveness

# Detailed Breakdown

## وَإِنَّ ٱلسَّاعَةَ لَـَٔاتِيَةٌۭ
- basic translation: "and definitely, the hour is certainly coming"

### إِنَّ
- the "inna" adds the first emphasis - "definitely"

### ٱلسَّاعَةَ
- basic translation: the hour

### لَ 
- the lam adds an additional layer of emphasis

### اتِيَةٌۭ 
- the noun form is used for the idea of "coming/arriving", rather than the verb form of "coming/arriving"
- the use of the noun over the verb adds another layer of emphasis [Noun and Verbs for Emphasis](/others/grammar/nouns-verbs-emphasis.md)

### Summary
- so overall, this sentence has several layers of emphasis - that would not make sense in a basic english translation
- a possible closer translation would be something like this: "and definitely, the hour is an Arriver" 
  - with the understanding that "Arriver" as a noun adds a higher level of emphasis than "Arriving" as the verb

## فَٱصْفَحِ ٱلصَّفْحَ ٱلْجَمِيلَ
