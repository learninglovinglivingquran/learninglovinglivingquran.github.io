# Introduction

## What is LQ?

Welcome to LQ.

The goal of this resource is to facilitate what we have termed "Quranic Manifestion".

What is Quranic Manifestion?

Quranic Manifestion refers to the idea that when you truly need it, the Quran will be made manifest for you in a way that will seem customized for you and your situation.

Why would you want that?

The goal for facilitating Quranic Manifestion is that by doing so, it will signifcantly improve your life, again, in a way that will seem almost tailor-made for you. Whether its consolation through a tough time, mentorship when you don't know what to do, or even tough love when you need it, if you approach the Quran as a sincere seeker looking for truth, the Quran will be made manifest for you and it will make things better.

How can you do it?

To facilitate Quranic Manifestion, we should begin to have a consistent, intentional connection or engagement with the Quran, even if it's little. The idea is to let your inner conscience, your soul, connect with the Quran, engage with it regularly, with the hope that as you live life and when you need help, the Quran will made manifest for you as you need.

## What is the minimum requirements?

There are two minimum requirements from you, which the Quran itself will clarify as you begin.

The first minimum requirement is an admission and acceptance of your own ignorance about life. You must come to the Quran admitting and accepting that you don't know everything about life.

To be clear, the requirement is not that you must accept what the Quran tells you blindly because you don't know. The requirement is that you come to it admitting and accepting you don't know, and you are willing to consider what the Quran has to offer.

The second minimum requirement is that you are cautious when confronted with things you don't know (i.e. life). This means that when you are in an environment outside of your knowledge, you think it's better to be careful and cautious as you proceed, rather than being careless or reckless about it.

To be clear, the requirement is not that you don't do or not do anything because you don't know everything about everything. The requirement is just that before you do or not do something, you are careful about it, and do not make decisions recklessly.
