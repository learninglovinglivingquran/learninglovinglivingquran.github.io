# Language for my Master
to deepen our appreciation of the quran, we have to add multiple dimensions of appreciation. while the primary way of appreciating the quran should be the role it plays in our daily lives when we live with the quran, another very important aspect of appreciating the quran is to deepen the appreciation of its language, and appreciate it as an arabic quran.

in this section, we will learn aspects of arabic that will help us in that journey. note that you will not learn arabic here. what you will learn are bits and pieces you should know to deepen the realization of what the quran is.

# basic topics
- ism
  - important because these are the objects and ideas
  - definition
  - 'irab - status
    - important because it controls meaning
  - 'adad - number
    - important because it adds depth to meaning
    - sometimes you expect single, and you see multiple, or vice versa
  - jins - gender
    - important because it adds depth to meaning
    - sometimes you expect masculine, and you see feminine and vice versa
  - qism - type
    - important because it adds depth to meaning
    - sometimes you expect specific, and you see general, or vice versa
- fi'l
  - important because they show the relations between objects and ideas
  - definition
- harf
  - important because they show the nuances of the relations between objects and ideas
  - definition
- pronouns
  - important because unexpected changes in pronouns enhance meaning
  - definition
- jaar wa majroor
  - important because it clarifies meaning
  - definition
- harf nasb wa ismuhaa
  - important because it clarifies meaning
  - definition
- idhaafa
  - important because it shows possession
  - definition
- mowsoof wa sifah
  - important because it adds details to the ism
  - definition
- ism ishara wa mushaarun ilaih
  - important because it adds literal and/or symbolic meaning
  - definition
- harf 'atf
  - important because it enhances meaning by its presence or absence
  - definition

- jumlah ismyyah
  - important because it focuses on the ism in the sentence
  - definition
  - invisible is
  - 'irab
  - abnormal sentence structure
  - HE HAS and THERE IS sentences
  - complex sentences

- fi'l maadhi
  - important because it shows a past or past continuous relationship between objects or ideas - also emphasizes single occurrence
  - definition
  - faa'il
  - maf'ool
  - abnormal sentence structure
  - complex sentences
  - question words

- fi'l mudhaari'
  - important because it shows a present or present continuous or future relationship between objects or ideas - also symbolizes repeated actions
  - definition
  - fi'l mudhaari' associated with huruf
  - commanding and forbidding
  - abnormal sentence structure
  - noon al-tawkeed
  - distinguishing between asmaa' and af'aal

- fi'l mabni lil majhool
  - important because of rhetorical enhancement in meaning
  - definition
  - sentence structure
  - transivity

- negation
  - important because there are multiple types of negation - so the type used enhances the meaning
  - negating jumlah fi'lyyah
  - negating jumlah ismyyah
  - shared tools of negation

- af'aal naqisah
  - important because they show a special type of relationship between objects and ideas
  - definition
  - sentence structure
  - abnormal sentence structure
  - complex sentence structure
  - translating kaana sentences
  - negating kaana setences

- compound ism
  - important because they allow for complete sentences to be used as ism - allowing greater depth in meaning
  - definition
  - ann and anna
  - tools that create compound mudhaaf ilaih
